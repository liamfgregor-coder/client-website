About Us

Your trusted partner in beautiful interiors.

Nestled in the heart of Windsor, Invercargill, Interior Girls has been a cornerstone of Southland's design scene for more than two decades. We're a local, owner-operated business built on a genuine love of beautiful spaces.

From your first call to the final fitting, we guide you through every decision — offering expert advice, premium products, and a seamless full service from concept to completion. No stress, no guesswork, just results you'll love.
